Questions & Answers

Does it support multi-device pairing? ▼
Yes, the Zenith X1 Pro supports Multipoint Bluetooth, allowing you to connect to two devices simultaneously and switch between them seamlessly.
Is the battery user-replaceable? ▼
The battery is designed for long-term use (over 1000 charge cycles). While not user-replaceable, we offer a specialized battery service via our support team.
Can I use them with a wire? ▼
Absolutely. Every Zenith X1 comes with a premium 3.5mm braided audio cable for passive listening when the battery is low.
